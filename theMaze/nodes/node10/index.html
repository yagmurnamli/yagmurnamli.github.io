Cleaning the dirty dishes takes a few minutes, but you soon realize it was worth it.
When you place the last clean glass back on the shelf, you hear a voice from above.

Traveler… I am grateful. You have freed me from what was holding my soul in this realm. Those dirty dishes, I never had the time to wash them before my death. They were the key to my redemption! Accept my gift. I will free you from this twisted maze. As my soul rises to heaven, I will carry you out of this trap.

Your act of kindness is rewarded with freedom. You feel your body growing lighter, and soon you begin to levitate above the walls of the maze.
