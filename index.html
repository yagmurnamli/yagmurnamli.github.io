Experimental Games by VYNA

BUNRAKU BUNRAKU is a stylized narrative game about exploring surreal spaces and fragmented stories in a neon-lit world. THE MAZE THE MAZE challenges your spatial awareness through increasingly complex puzzles in a minimalist digital labyrinth. WHAT'S IN MY BACKYARD? Discover hidden stories and secrets in a backyard environment full of surreal surprises and playful experiments. MASK (BUNRAKU PROTOTYPE VERSION) A prototype continuation of BUNRAKU focusing on identity, masks, and perception in abstract interactive spaces. PULSATION (EXPERIMENTAL TYPEFACE) An experimental typeface game that visualizes rhythm and pulsation in a dynamic textual interface.
